Our History

Dr. Raja Super Specialty Hospital began as an inspiring story of dedication that started with Dr. Raja Achlerkar. Deeply influenced by the hardships he witnessed treating patients in tribal regions of Dhule, he committed himself to delivering essential healthcare where it was needed most — travelling to remote tribal hamlets in Taloda to serve the community.

His wife, Dr. Chitrarekha, played a significant role in this mission, treating women for eighteen years. After personally reviving a bitten patient in a tribal village, the family's commitment to patient care only deepened.

Inspired by his father's legacy, Rahul pursued his M.B.B.S. and specialised in ophthalmology, completing a fellowship at the Aravind Eye Hospital. After gaining experience in Kerala, Dr. Noopur returned to Maharashtra with him to found the Dr. Raja Super Specialty Eye Hospital near Nerul Station.

Today, the hospital offers a wide range of treatments — cataract surgery, non-surgical strabismus care, and specialised care for diabetic patients — continuing a legacy of resilience and compassion in healthcare.
